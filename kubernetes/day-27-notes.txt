Day 27 - Kubernetes Troubleshooting
===================================

Useful Commands

kubectl get pods

kubectl describe pod <pod-name>

kubectl logs <pod-name>

kubectl exec -it <pod-name> -- /bin/sh

kubectl get events

kubectl get deployments

kubectl get svc

kubectl delete pod <pod-name>

Common Errors

ImagePullBackOff
- Wrong image name.
- Private registry issue.

CrashLoopBackOff
- Application crashes repeatedly.
- Incorrect startup command.

Pending
- Not enough CPU or Memory.
- Scheduler unable to place Pod.

Wrong Service Selector
- Labels do not match Deployment.

Port Mismatch
- targetPort does not match containerPort.

Learning Outcome

• Learned common Kubernetes errors.
• Understood debugging commands.
• Learned to inspect Pods and Services.
