Day 25 - Kubernetes Pods, Deployments & Services
================================================

Topics Covered

• Pod
- Smallest deployable unit in Kubernetes.
- Contains one or more containers.
- Gets its own IP address.

• Deployment
- Manages Pods.
- Supports scaling and rolling updates.
- Automatically recreates failed Pods.

• Service
- Exposes Pods inside or outside the cluster.
- Provides a stable endpoint.
- Types:
  - ClusterIP
  - NodePort
  - LoadBalancer

Commands Learned

kubectl create deployment nginx-demo --image=nginx

kubectl get deployments

kubectl get pods

kubectl expose deployment nginx-demo --type=NodePort --port=80

kubectl get svc

kubectl scale deployment nginx-demo --replicas=3

Learning Outcome
